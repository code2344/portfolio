    • Service

    The purpose of engaging in Service is to develop empathy and teamwork. It instills a sense of responsibility, builds character and contributes to community betterment. Recognising our ability to do things for others, using our gifts and talents, helps us appreciate our how lucky we are and understand more about the needs of others 

    Key aspects:

  • undertake a service activity of choice that involves 20 hours of committment.
  • Include a minimum of 5 hours in each areas of CAS (Creativity, Action & Service) 

    • Hopefully, you learned a lot about your service opportunity, the people that run the organisation, those in need and what a positive impact volunteers such as yourself can have. 

    Explain the things you learned and why this service provider is so valuable to the Community.

    Explain the full process how you went about your service activity. Who and how did you contact, what did you actually do, what were the desired outcomes, was it a success, would you recommend this to others, can you see yourself being involved in organisation / service this in the future?
