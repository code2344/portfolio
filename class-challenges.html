Class Challenges

Challenge 1. The Staves

Key aspects:

  • Human tendency is to focus on the next thing and not pay attention to the present. 
  • Moving on quickly to the next thing rather than making sure what you leave behind is a ‘reasonable state’ is important.

    • Reflection 

  • How did it feel to achieve the simple challenge relative to the feeling after a difficult challenge?
  • How did it feel to achieve a challenge alone compared to as a member of a group?

    • Challenge 2. Learning to 'Juggle'

    Key aspects:

  • Learning to juggle can help you to appreciate ‘failure’ is a stepping stone on the path to success. 
  • ‘This is me juggling’ in recognition that this is your ‘performance’ in this moment. 
  • Abilities can be developed through dedication and hard work (growth mindset), they are not fixed.
