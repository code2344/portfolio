Work Experience

As part of our Year 10 Career Development program, all Year 10 students have the opportunity to participate in Work Experience in Week 10 of Term 3. 

This is a fabulous opportunity for students to get an insight into the world of work in a field of their choice. 

There are no formal classes during this week.
