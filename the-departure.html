The Departure

The purpose of this event was for students along with their guardians to celebrate childhood and recognise that physically, socially, and mentally they have started to develop into young adults. Acknowledge that students need to start taking more responsibility for their actions and behaviours.

Key aspects:

  • guardians come into school with some momentos to acknowledge their child's childhood and to formally recognise their transition into becoming young adults.
  • students share a gratitude letter to their guardians. 

    • 1) What was your highlight from this experience? 

    2) Was it what you were expecting?  Easier or harder?

    3) Can you highlight any key things that your families shared that either triggered an emotion or potentially reminded you of a positive experience?

    4) What was your family's response to your letter? Did you have the courage to read it? How did that make you feel? Was there any specific part that was emotion-inducing? Why?

    5) If you did it again, what would you do differently?
