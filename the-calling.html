    • The Calling

    The purpose of this event was to promote community and connection and provide students with an opportunity to get to know each other better. 

    Key aspects:

  • team work, challenge and creative workshops
  • sleep overnight at school
  • sunrise on Kunanyi and walk back to school

    • 1) What was your highlight from this experience?

    2) Was it what you were expecting?  Easier or harder?

    3) Did you make any new connections with different people? If not, can you challenge yourself to this in 2026?

     4) If you did it again, what would you do differently?

    5) Reflect on a personal challenge or obstacle you faced during your The Calling. How did you overcome it and what did you learn from it?

    6) How do you plan to move forward from what you ‘let go’?
