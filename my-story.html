Ruben Sutton - My Story

The purpose of this activity was to recognise that 'Connection requires vulnerability and the courage to be authentic and genuine’ quote Brene Brown. 

Key aspects:

  • preparation and sharing of 'My Story' Presentation.

    • What parts of this challenge did you enjoy? Highlight an aspect that brought up positive memories.

    Were there any challenges or things you found difficult to reflect on? How have they impacted the person you are now and what have you learned from them?

    How did you feel presenting about your childhood experiences and listening to others? Explain the main emotions you experienced.
